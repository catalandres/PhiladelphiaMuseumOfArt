	<h2>Search Tips</h2>
		<ul>
			<li>Not getting the amount of results expected? Try using the * after your keyword to broaden your search results.<br/>
			<li>Getting too many matches? Try using " " to limit your results to match an exact phrase.<br/>
			<li>Refer to the examples of how to use the above mentioned operators.</li>
		</ul>

<b>Examples:</b>  
		<ul>
			<li>apple banana<br/>
			Find results that contain at least one of the two words.</li> 
			<li>apple*<br/>
			Find results that contain words such as `apple`, `apples`, `applesauce`, or `applet`.</li> 
			<li>"some words"<br/>
			Find results that contain the exact phrase `some words` (for example, results that contain `some words of wisdom` but not `some wise words`).</li> 
			<li>+apple +juice<br/>
			Find results that contain both words.</li>
			<li>+apple macintosh<br/>
			Find results that contain the word `apple`, but rank results higher if they also contain `macintosh`.</li> 
			<li>+apple -macintosh<br/>
			Find results that contain the word `apple` but not `macintosh`.</li>
			<li>+apple +(>turnover &lt;strudel)<br/>
			Find rows that contain the words `apple` and `turnover`, or `apple` and `strudel` (in any order), but rank `apple turnover` higher than `apple strudel`. 
		</ul>
