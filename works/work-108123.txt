<div id="recordData"><p><i><strong>Wall Pocket</strong></i></p><p>In the second panel from the top, an angel displays the coats of arms of a Swiss family; the other panels depict seven allegorical female figures with their attributes.  From top to bottom and left to right they are: Hope (anchor); Prudence (snake and mirror) and Temperance (jug and cup); Strength (column) and Mercy (burning heart); Faith (book and cross) and Justice (sword and balance).  The figures are dressed in the fashions of about 1685-1715.</p><p>Artist/maker unknown, Swiss</p><p></p><div align="left" style="padding-bottom:6px; padding-top:14px; border-top:1px
