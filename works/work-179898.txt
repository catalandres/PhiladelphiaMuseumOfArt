<div id="recordData"><p><i><strong>Pitcher</strong></i></p><p>(Left of handle) Two images, the upper image features the caption "Shave for a Penny, Hair Drest For Two Pence, And A Glass of Gin into the Bargain," and shows three men receiving various beauty treatments. The lower image in an oval medallion depicts a boy and girl walking hand-in-hand.
(Right of handle) An image with the title "The Gipsy Fortune Teller" features a woman with a baby bundled on her back holding and looking at the hand of a seated woman while a man, standing behind the seated woman, observes.</p><p>Artist/maker unknown, English.
