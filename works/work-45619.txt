<div id="recordData"><p><i><strong>Royal Oak Goblet</strong></i></p><p>The front is engraved with the head of Charles II of England superimposed on the branches of an oak tree along with the words ROYAL OAK.  Engraved on the sides are portraits of Charles II and his wife, Catherine of Braganza, and on the back the coat of arms of Charles II, the words DIEV ET MON DROIT (God and my right), and the date 1663.</p><p>Artist/maker unknown, English.
